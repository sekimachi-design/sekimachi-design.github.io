Features

Everything in one place

No switching between five apps. Vitality covers workouts, food, sleep, and mindfulness — unified and simple.

Workout Tracking

Log sets, reps, and cardio. Follows your program and auto-adjusts weight based on your recent performance.

Nutrition Planner

Scan barcodes, track macros, and get meal suggestions that match your goals, dietary needs, and taste.

Mindfulness & Sleep

Guided meditations, breathwork, and sleep tracking. Wind down with audio sessions designed for recovery days.

Progress Insights

Weekly reports, personal records, and streak tracking. Understand what's working and what to adjust.
