Our Story

Take It Slow,
Like Gazing at the Sky

SORA Coffee was born in 2019 in the heart of Melbourne, with the concept of offering a relaxed time — like gazing up at the sky.

We carefully hand-drip each cup using specialty coffee beans selected from around the world. Together with our homemade sweets, we offer a peaceful escape from everyday life.
