About

Designer who codes.
Developer who designs.

I'm a UX Designer based in Tokyo with a unique background spanning both design and development. Starting as a programmer, I transitioned into UX design — giving me deep empathy for both users and the engineers building products.

Currently a Lead Designer at PERSOL CAREER, I previously worked as a contract UX Designer at Amazon Japan (via Aquent LLC) for 10 years, leading UX for 35+ projects including seasonal campaigns, category page redesigns, and service pages — always grounding decisions in quantitative data and user research.

I'm passionate about accessibility, and actively educate teams on inclusive design practices. I also spent 4 years working in Australia, bringing a global perspective to my design thinking. I'm available for freelance projects through Sekimachi Design Studio.
